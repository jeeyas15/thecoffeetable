About The Coffee Table

Founded in 2015, The Coffee Table has grown from a small community initiative to a vital support network serving Wyndham Vale and surrounding areas.

Our Mission

To provide essential services while fostering a sense of community and belonging. We believe that everyone deserves access to basic necessities and human connection.

Our Vision

A community where no one faces hardship alone, where neighbors support neighbors, and where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Our History

What started as a weekly coffee morning in a local church basement has evolved into five specialized centers serving over 500 community members each week. Our growth reflects the genuine need for compassionate community support in our region.

Our Impact

  • 500+ Community Members Supported Weekly
  • 100+ Dedicated Volunteers
  • 5 Operating Centers Across the Region
  • Over 2,000 Meals Served Monthly
  • 50+ Success Stories Shared
