Our Services

Food Bank

Our Food Bank provides emergency food assistance to families and individuals in crisis. We distribute fresh produce, pantry staples, and hygiene items to over 200 households weekly.

Eligibility:
  • Residents of Wyndham Vale and surrounding areas
  • Valid ID and proof of address required
  • One visit per week per household
Hours:

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Community Kitchen

Our Community Kitchen offers hands-on cooking classes, meal preparation workshops, and communal dining experiences. Participants learn valuable life skills while building connections with others.

Programs:
  • Beginner Cooking Classes (Tuesdays & Thursdays)
  • Nutrition Education Workshops
  • Community Meal Preparation (Saturdays)
  • Cultural Cuisine Series
Hours:

Tuesday & Thursday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
Saturday: 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Coffee Table

More than just a place for a warm drink, our Coffee Table program provides a welcoming space for social connection, peer support groups, and community activities.

Activities:
  • Daily Coffee & Conversation (9:00 AM - 11:00 AM)
  • Peer Support Groups (Wednesdays)
  • Book Club & Reading Circle
  • Arts & Crafts Sessions
  • Guest Speakers & Workshops
Hours:

Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Sewing Classes

Our Sewing Classes teach practical skills that empower participants to create clothing, household items, and gifts while building confidence and creativity.

Class Offerings:
  • Beginner Sewing Fundamentals
  • Intermediate Techniques
  • Specialty Workshops (Quilting, Upholstery)
  • Community Projects (Blanket Making for Charity)
Schedule:

Monday & Wednesday: 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Friday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
