About The Coffee Table

Founded in 2015, The Coffee Table has grown from a small community initiative to a vital support network serving Wyndham Vale and surrounding areas.

Our mission is to provide essential services while fostering a sense of community and belonging. We operate five centers across the region, each offering tailored support to meet local needs.
